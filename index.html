CSE

Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 1994. The primary goal of CSE is to provide best IT infrastructure, world class learning & research environment, adopt industry practices through industry collaborations and inculcate moral and ethical values. The department is accredited by NBA.

ECE

Electronics and Communications Engineering (ECE) involves researching, designing, developing, and testing of electronic equipment used in various systems. Electronics and Communications engineers also conceptualize and oversee the manufacturing of communications and broadcast systems

EEE

Roles and responsibilities of EEE engineer: -They also focus on circuit design for electrically operated vehicles, digital devices, computers, electronic memory storage devices, electric cars and industrial robots. -They also build and operate the telecommunication systems, wireless and various internet networks.

MECH

Mechanical engineering is an engineering branch that combines engineering physics and mathematics principles with materials science, to design, analyze, manufacture, and maintain mechanical systems. It is one of the oldest and broadest of the engineering branches.

AUTOMOBILE

The study of automotive engineering is to design, develop, fabricate, and test vehicles or vehicle components from the concept stage to production stage. Production, development, and manufacturing are the three major functions in this field.
